Powerful Features

More Than Just a Panic Button

SOS-Connect provides multiple layers of security to alert contacts and authorities, even without an internet connection.

Voice Activation

Simply say "Help me" or a custom phrase to trigger alerts instantly when you can't reach your phone.

Smart Gestures

Trigger discreet alerts using unique patterns like double-tapping or shaking your phone in your pocket.

Timer-Based Alert

Set a countdown for risky situations (e.g., taxi rides). If not cancelled, it automatically sends an SOS.

Offline Tracking

No data? No problem. The app uses SMS and cellular triangulation to send your location coordinates.

IVR Verification

Receive an automated call to verify your safety. If you don't answer, emergency protocols are activated.

Auto Recording

Automatically captures audio and video evidence upon activation, stored securely in the cloud.
